WHAT IS NEBRASKA CANCER ASSOCIATION?
Established in 1993, the Nebraska cancer association is well-known for the support it provides for cancer victims. At NCA, we believe that the most effective medicine for any disease is COURAGE. So, we motivate our patients to be courageous and fight cancer through numerous programs, meetings, events conducted and volunteered by our volunteer army. Just like any nation's army that protects its safety, we protect our patients. We have so far helped 150+ patients suffering from more than 8 types of cancer to fight it and start a new life. We fill our patients with the hope to live a normal life, to enjoy every minute of it, and bravely fight cancer.Apart from providing support, we also fund cancer research programs with the donations collected from our generous donors and also raise funds through public events.
WHAT PROGRAMS DO WE CONDUCT?
NCA organizes numerous events - one-to-one meetings with patients, group events, and also public events which include but not limited to cancer awareness events, fundraising events, musical nights, motivational speeches. Patient motivation and counseling meetings are amongst the most important and frequent events conducted by us. Every patient is treated with at most care and attention their health is monitored on daily basis and appropriate actions are taken to ensure their wellness. All thanks to our wonderful volunteering team for coordinating these events.Today, our volunteer team base is as strong as 350 and still counting.
HOW CAN I ENROLL MYSELF AS A VOLUNTEER TO NCA?
Please use the SignUp page to register as a volunteer to our organization. Once you have entered all the details and submitted the online registration form, you will receive an email (to the email address provided during the registration) with username and one-time-password. Use these credentials to login to the website and change the password after the first login.
HOW WE ORGANIZE FUNDS? AND FOR WHAT?
NCA is a non-profit organization established with a single motto of supporting and motivating cancer patients to fight the deadly disease. So, every single cent of the fund we receive is spent on the facilities we provide for our patients. We receive funds from donations given by the public, government agencies, and private organizations, and also through fundraising events. These funds are spent to organize events, maintain the organization, and fund the cancer research programs. NCA has been awarded the best cancer association in Nebraska and is also well recognized for the work we do. The cancer research program that we fund updates us on every progress they make and every cent that is spent. Our volunteers work for the welfare of our patient instead of salary, making them selfless people and true heroes.
HOW TO ENROLL A PATIENT TO OUR ORGANIZATION?
If you need our assistance with cancer patients, please reach out to us through phone or email mentioned in the contact us page. Average response time to the emails is 2-3 business days. We request you to avoid visiting our office due to the COVID-19 pandemic situation.
DO PATIENTS NEED TO PAY FEE TO ENROLL?
NO, there is no enrollment fee for patients to join us.
WHAT ROLE DO VOLUNTEERS PLAY?
Volunteers are the key to the success of our organization. Events, patients counselling meetings (one-to-one meetings or group meetings) are handled by the volunteer under the supervision of NCA staff. Volunteers are required to maintain patient records confidential.
